Fahim Faisal Niloy

I am a third year PhD Student at the Vision and Learning Group of University of California, Riverside. I am supervised by Dr. Amit K. Roy-Chowdhury. My PhD research focuses on efficient adaptation of foundation models, with particular emphasis on parameter efficient tuning, test-time adaptation, and optimizing deployment on edge devices. Additionally, I have strong interest in media forensics, specifically in areas such as image and video forgery localization and deepfake detection.
